Bible Aura's Commitment to Privacy: We are dedicated to protecting your personal information and Bible study data. This privacy policy explains how Bible Aura collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use our AI-powered Bible study platform.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create a Bible Aura account, we collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Data: Optional profile picture, spiritual preferences, study goals
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials and session information

Bible Study Data

To provide personalized Bible Aura experiences, we collect:

  • Bible AI Chat Conversations: Your questions and AI responses for improving service
  • Study Progress: Reading history, completed studies, favorite verses
  • Journal Entries: Personal reflections and notes (encrypted and private)
  • Usage Analytics: Feature usage, session duration, popular content

Technical Information

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Location Data: General location for content localization (optional)
  • Cookies and Tracking: Session cookies, preferences, analytics data

2. How Bible Aura Uses Your Data

Primary Uses

  • Personalized Bible Study: Tailoring AI responses to your spiritual journey
  • Account Management: Creating and maintaining your Bible Aura profile
  • Service Improvement: Enhancing AI accuracy and platform features
  • Communication: Sending important updates about Bible Aura services
  • Support: Providing customer service and technical assistance

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Consent: When you voluntarily provide information
  • Contract Performance: To provide Bible Aura services you've requested
  • Legitimate Interest: For service improvement and analytics
  • Legal Compliance: When required by applicable law

3. Bible AI Chat Privacy

Important: Your Bible AI chat conversations are treated with special care. While we use conversation data to improve our AI responses, your personal spiritual questions and discussions remain confidential.

How We Handle AI Conversations

  • Privacy by Design: Conversations are processed securely and not shared with third parties
  • AI Training: Anonymized conversation patterns help improve Bible AI accuracy
  • Personal Questions: Sensitive spiritual inquiries are specially protected
  • Data Retention: Conversations are stored securely for service improvement
  • User Control: You can delete conversation history at any time

What We Don't Do

  • Share your personal Bible questions with other users
  • Use your conversations for marketing to third parties
  • Provide your spiritual data to religious organizations without consent
  • Sell your personal Bible study information

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

When Bible Aura Shares Information

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help operate Bible Aura (under strict contracts)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Safety Protection: To protect users' safety or Bible Aura's security
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger or acquisition (with privacy protections)

Third-Party Services

Bible Aura integrates with:

  • Analytics Services: Google Analytics (anonymized data)
  • Authentication: Google OAuth (minimal data sharing)
  • Payment Processing: Secure payment providers (PayHere, Stripe)
  • Cloud Storage: Encrypted data storage (Supabase)

5. Security Measures

How Bible Aura Protects Your Data

  • Encryption: All data transmitted and stored is encrypted
  • Access Controls: Strict employee access limitations
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Secure Infrastructure: Industry-standard hosting and security protocols
  • Password Protection: Strong password requirements and secure storage

Your Security Responsibilities

  • Keep your Bible Aura password secure and private
  • Log out of shared devices after using Bible Aura
  • Report any suspicious activity to our support team
  • Keep your account information up to date

6. Your Rights and Choices

Data Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

  • Access: Request copies of your Bible Aura data
  • Correction: Update or correct your personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your Bible study data
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Objection: Object to certain data processing activities

How to Exercise Your Rights

Contact us at privacy@bibleaura.xyz or through your Bible Aura account settings. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Account Controls

  • Privacy Settings: Control data sharing preferences
  • Communication Preferences: Manage email notifications
  • Data Export: Download your Bible study data
  • Account Deletion: Permanently delete your Bible Aura account

7. Cookies and Tracking

Types of Cookies Bible Aura Uses

  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

  • Marketing Cookies: Provide relevant content and features

Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect Bible Aura functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

Bible Aura is designed for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will delete it immediately.

Parental Guidance: We encourage parents to supervise their children's use of Bible Aura and discuss online safety and spiritual growth together.
